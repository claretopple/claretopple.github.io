TAS Syllabus

My name is Clare ToppleÂ and I am a Design and Technology pre-service teacher. I am currently completing a Bachelor of Education (Secondary)/Bachelor of Arts (Technology) degree at the Australian Catholic University. I have come to this degree as a mature age student, after many years working in the hospitality industry in training and development, running a cake decorating business, a love for all things textiles and craft and personal family experience with children who have learning difficulties. This, combined with my passion for the food and textiles industries, has led me to this degree and a future in teaching. I am now striving to become a Design and Technology Teacher who supports, inspires and encourages creative and innovative future designers.

The teacher I want to be

From my past experiences, passions and hobbies I want to be a teacher who supports all learners regardless of level of ability or disability or learning difficulty, one that can help nurture a love for learning, encourage students to do their best and teach them new skills and knowledge. I want to inspire students, help develop and foster innovative and critical thinkers and encourage a life long passion for learning.
